AI Benchmarking STT¶

This is a collaborative project to create a library for benchmarking AI/ML applications. It evolved out of conversations among broadcasters and providers of Access Services to media organisations, but anyone is welcome to contribute. The group behind this project is the EBU’s Media Information Management & AI group. Currently the group is focussing on Speech-to-Text, but it will consider creating benchmarking tools for other AI/ML services.

For general information about this project, including the motivations and guiding principles, please see the project wiki and documentation.
